# Ideate

Help the developer brainstorm and explore ideas for the next iteration. Facilitate an open-ended ideation session to surface potential features, improvements, or product directions before committing to a formal requirement definition.

## Instructions

1. Ask the developer what problem or opportunity they want to explore.
2. Help generate and organise ideas through structured brainstorming.
3. Summarise the top candidate ideas with brief pros/cons.
4. Identify which idea is most promising to carry forward into `nvst define requirement`.
